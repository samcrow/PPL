Finds an airport with the given ID and adds its position to the cache.

Also adds the positions of other airports that are found on the way.

If the requested airport is not found, it is not added to the cache.

Parameters
mapA reference to a mapping from airport codes to stream positions. Airports will be inserted into this map as they are found.
promisesA reference to a mapping from airport codes to promises. When an airport is found with a valid promise, the promise wil be fulfulled with the airport's position in the file
